✦ Who We Are

More Than Just a Shop

Thrift & Thread started as a weekend hobby — hunting for hidden gems at flea markets and crafting tiny keychains at the kitchen table. What began as a passion project quickly grew into something beautiful: a community of people who believe fashion should be sustainable, personal, and full of character.

Every thrifted piece we carry has been hand-selected for its quality, uniqueness, and style. Every keychain is made by hand, with care, and with you in mind.
